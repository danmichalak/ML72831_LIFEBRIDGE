How does it work?

Once you're enrolled, the plan is simple to use.

For covered services you can simply call  and say "legal" or visit www.info.legalplans.com to find a Network Attorney where you need one, and to obtain a case number.

You then call the Network Attorney you select to schedule an appointment at a time that's convenient for you.

It's that simple. You may also choose to use an attorney who is not in the network; in this case you will be reimbursed according to a set fee schedule.

What types of services are covered?
  • Estate planning documents, including wills and trusts
  • Real estate matters
  • Identity theft
  • Financial matters, such as debt-collection defense
  • Traffic offenses
  • Document review
  • Family law, including adoption and name change
  • Advice and consultation on personal legal matters
  • And more.
